About

I am a Junior Computer Engineering major at Texas A&M Univeristy based out of Houston, Texas.

Despite my major, my relationship with coding and software hasn't always been smooth. My first experience with coding was by way of AP Computer Science in highschool. Although I tried my best in the class, something just didn't click as I found myself struggling to keep up with the material and losing interest. As a result of this, I entered Texas A&M as a biomedical engineer as I had no interest in software related majors. Fortunatly for me, my first semester of university, I took a mandatory Introduction to Python course. I remember being captivated by the different projects and assignments; and as a result, I learned alot while enjoying myself too. I did my research, asked people in the field for advice, and decided to apply to become a Computer Engineer with a focus on Computer Science. I'm happy to say that I haven't looked back since! :)
